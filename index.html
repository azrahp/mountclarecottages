Faith-Conscious Modest Luxury For Young Muslim Women

Situated at 2 Mountclare in the peaceful enclave of Sybrand Park, Cape Town, Mountclare Cottages is a newly built, boutique student residence crafted specifically for female UCT students who desire an uncompromised standard of living.

We provide a warm, secure, and faith-inspired environment where academic focus thrives alongside personal peace of mind. Every detail—from our fully furnished suites to our full meal plan and daily private transport—has been thoughtfully curated for discerning parents and ambitious students.
